Step into the Future of Streaming: How to Host an Epic Teleparty

Is setting up a compatible web browser and installing the Teleparty extension all it takes? Think again! There are several simple yet crucial steps to create the perfect watch party. So, don't delay—start making a list of your top 100 friends to invite. Let's get this party started:

  • IDive into Teleparty Ready to elevate your streaming experience? Begin by installing the Teleparty extension. Ensure you use a supported web browser—Google Chrome or Microsoft Edge—and have a compatible device such as a Windows PC, Chromebook, or macOS. Click here to install and dive into the world of shared streaming!
  • Pin Your Teleparty Extension Next up, pin the Teleparty extension to your browser’s toolbar for easy access. This will place the extension icon right next to your address bar. If you don't see the TP button, click the puzzle icon near the URL bar and add the TP button to your toolbar from there. Moreover, this will position it in the top right corner of your screen, ready for action.
  • Sign In to Your Streaming Account Simultaneously, remember to sign in to your streaming account by visiting your favorite streaming site, such as Disney Plus, Netflix, Hulu, Hotstar, HBO Max, or any other service you prefer. Furthermore, both hosts and participants need to have their own subscriptions and login accounts to join the watch party.
  • Choose, Queue, and Enjoy Watching your favorite videos with friends is the ultimate delight. To get started, pick the video you want to share at your watch party. Begin playing the video, then pause it momentarily—this step is essential to setting up your watch party.
  • Generate Your Teleparty Link This step is critical: creating a Teleparty URL to launch your watch party. Click the TP icon next to your browser’s address bar. This will open the "Create A Watch Party" window with a "Start a Party" button. Click this button to generate a watch party link to share with your friends.
  • Join the Teleparty Friends can join the watch party by clicking the Teleparty invitation link. If you're joining, make sure you also have the Teleparty extension installed. Click the watch party link, and you'll be taken straight to the party. Once there, you can enjoy the movie or show together and chat with your friends during the screening.
